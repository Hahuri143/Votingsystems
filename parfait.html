Sweety Strawberry parfait :D

Ingredients:

  • 1 package organic unsweetened oatmeal
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 3/4 cups of Cocoa
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt(or yogurt of choice)
  • 1/3 cup granola(use your favorite brand)
  • 1/3 cup blackberries


Steps

  1. Heat 1 package organic unsweetened oatmeal according to package directions
  2. While oatmeal is warming, slice half of the strawberries length wise.
  3. Place half of the strawberries in the bottom of each of two jars (or bowls).
  4. Dice the other half of the strawberries and set aside.
  5. When oatmeal is done cooking, divide it evenly between the two jars.
  6. Next add the Greek yogurt layer to each jar placing half in each.
  7. Sprinkle the granola on top of the Greek yogurt.
  8. Finish off with diced strawberries and fresh blackberries.
