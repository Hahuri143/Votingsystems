Delish Choco Cake :D

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups of Cocoa
  • 1 1/2 tsps of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tsps of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of boiling water


Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Stir together 2 cups of sugar, 1 3/4 cups of flour, 3/4 cup of the cocoa, 1 1/2 tsps baking powder, 1 1/2 tsps baking soda and 1 tsp salt in large bowl.
  3. Add 2 eggs, 1 cup of milk, 1/2 cup of vegetable oil and 2 tsps vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in 1 cup of boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
  8. Frost with Perfectly Chocolate Frosting.
